Indian Dishes Recipe(Paneer Butter Masala)

Indian dishes are much more than just spices and curry. They give you a taste of the rich culture and cooking acumen possessed by Indians.

Total Time : 45-50 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 Packet Paneer
  • 1 Tsp Garam masala
  • 1 Tsp cumin seeds
  • 100 gm Tomato
  • 100 gm Onion

Preparation

  1. Take a packet of paneer and cut it into pieces as per your choice.

    Time: 5 minutes

  2. Then, take a little amount of oil in a kadai and heat it at medium flame.

    Time: 15 minutes

  3. Put some finely chopped vegies in it and let it cook.

    Time: 15 minutes

  4. Finally, add some garam masala and cumin seeds to elevate the flavour.

    Time: 15 minutes
